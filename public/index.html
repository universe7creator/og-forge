Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How do I use OG Forge? +
Simply call our API with your desired parameters. For example: /api/og?title=My+Post&template=blog&theme=dark Add the resulting URL to your og:image meta tag.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes! You can test the API with limited requests before purchasing. The demo above shows exactly what your images will look like.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and crypto via LemonSqueezy. One-time payment means no recurring charges, ever.
Can I use this for commercial projects? +
Absolutely! Your purchase includes unlimited commercial use. Use OG Forge for as many projects as you like.
How long does image generation take? +
Images are generated in under 100ms thanks to Vercel Edge Functions. Images are then cached at the edge for 24 hours.
